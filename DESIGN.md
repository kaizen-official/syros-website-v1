# SYROS Healthcare Brand Guidelines

---

# A Note on the Brand

## A Brand Built for the Moments That Matter

SYROS exists to bring metro-grade critical care closer to home.

Our identity must read as **advanced, ordered, and human** - never cold, never clinical for its own sake.

This document is the operating manual for that identity. It defines how SYROS looks, sounds, and behaves across every surface - from an ambulance door to a discharge summary.

Use it as a guardrail, not a cage.

> Healing is a blessing that flourishes where compassion, expertise, and integrity come together.

---

# Brand Essence

## Purpose

To nurture healthcare institutions that improve lives and serve communities with unwavering care.

## Tagline

**Healing is a blessing**

To partner with healthcare providers to deliver excellence in patient care, governance, technology, and operational performance.

To become the most trusted healthcare management partner, bringing compassionate and reliable care to every individual we serve across India.

Tertiary-grade critical care, brought closer to Tier-2 and Tier-3 India - engineered with the precision of a global health system, delivered with the warmth of a neighbourhood doctor.

## Typography Rules

### 1. Tracked, Not Loud

- Display: -20 to -30 tracking
- Labels: +160 to +280 tracking
- Body: default tracking

### 2. Pretty Wraps

Use:

```css
text-wrap: pretty;
```

Avoid:

- Orphans
- Ladders
- Rivers

### 3. Italic Is a Tool

Use Newsreader italic only once per layout.

### 4. No All-Caps in Body Text

All-caps reserved for:

- Mono labels
- Section codes

### 5. Numerals

- Oldstyle figures in body text
- Tabular figures only in tables/forms

### 6. One Italic Per Page

If one italic display word is used, everything else remains upright.

# Voice & Tone

## Core Principles

### Do

- Use the patient's name once
- Lead with the answer
- Explain the reasoning
- Clearly state next steps
- Be specific

### Don't

- Use exclamation marks in clinical communications
- Bury conclusions
- Use marketing superlatives
- Sound overly enthusiastic

### We Write

> "Your CT scan was clear. We'd like to see you again in six months for a routine follow-up."

### We Don't Write

> "Great news! Your scan results are absolutely fantastic - we can't wait to see you again soon!"

### Voice Summary

**Plain. Steady. Specific.**
